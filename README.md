## Requirements

- Minimum node version 20.x.
- Minimum NPM version 10.

## Dependencies

- Requires at least: 6.7
- Tested up to: 6.9
- Requires PHP: 7.4

## Get started

This extension requires a Polako Finance merchant account. [Contact us to sign up](https://polako-finance.com).

## How does it work?

At checkout, customers are automatically taken to the bank page through Polako Finance to pay for their order.
Once payment is complete, Polako Finance redirects them to your store to continue shopping.

## External services

This plugin sends the order information to the [Polako Finance](https://polako-finance.com) API to process payments.
[Privacy Policy](https://polako-finance.com/privacy).

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### Where can I find documentation?

You don't need one. When you sign up for a merchant account, we set you up for free.

#### Where can I get support?

Get in touch via the [website](https://polako-finance.com).

#### Does this extension support subscriptions?

Not yet, but they will be available shortly.

#### Do I need an SSL certificate?

Yes, a [Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) certificate](https://woocommerce.com/document/ssl-and-https) is required for additional customer security and secure communication with our system.

#### Can I accept international payments?

Yes! You can receive Visa, Mastercard, and DinaCard payments from anywhere in the world.

#### Do I need a Polako Finance account?

Yes; a [merchant account](https://polako-finance.com) is required.

#### Do I need to be a Serbian resident to open a merchant account?

No, but to open a merchant account, you'll need a Serbian business bank account to pay your funds into.
